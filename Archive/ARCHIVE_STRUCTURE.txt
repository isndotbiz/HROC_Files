LEGEND:
-------
[KEEP]              = Active document, current version
[TO ARCHIVE]        = Superseded version, move to Archive/
[PERMANENT]         = Never destroy, archive when needed
[7 YEAR RETENTION]  = Keep 7 years, then destroy per disposal log
[3 YEAR RETENTION]  = Keep 3 years, then destroy per disposal log
[REVIEW]            = Determine if duplicate or separate version
[UPDATE NEEDED]     = Requires correction/updating
[MISSING]           = Critical document not yet created/located
[COMPLETED]         = Task finished
[IN PROGRESS]       = Task underway
[PENDING]           = Task not started
*** CRITICAL ***    = High priority missing document
