DISPOSAL LOGS DIRECTORY
========================

Purpose:
--------
This directory maintains records of all documents destroyed according to HROC's
Document Retention & Destruction Policy (adopted July 27, 2025).

Per the policy, a log of disposed documents MUST be maintained permanently, even
though the documents themselves are destroyed.

Files in this Directory:
------------------------
1. disposal_log_template.csv - Template for creating disposal logs
2. disposal_log_YYYY.csv - Annual disposal logs (one per year)

How to Use:
-----------
1. When a document reaches the end of its retention period, add an entry to the
   current year's disposal log (create new file if needed).

2. Before disposing of any document:
   - Verify retention period has elapsed
   - Confirm no litigation hold is in effect
   - Get authorization from Secretary or Chair
   - Have a witness present during destruction

3. After disposal:
   - Complete disposal log entry with all required fields
   - Both authorized person and witness should initial the log
   - Store certificate of destruction (if using shredding service)

Retention:
----------
Disposal logs are PERMANENT records and must never be destroyed.

Fields Required:
----------------
- Disposal_Date: When document was destroyed
- Document_Type: Category (Policy, Financial, Corporate, etc.)
- Document_Title: Full name/description of document
- Original_Date: When document was created
- Retention_Period: As specified in retention policy
- Retention_End_Date: Calculated end of retention
- Disposal_Method: How destroyed (shredding, deletion, etc.)
- Authorized_By: Officer who authorized disposal
- Witness: Person who witnessed destruction
- Notes: Reason, superseding document, or other relevant info

Disposal Methods Approved:
--------------------------
Paper Documents:
- Cross-cut shredding (on-site)
- Professional shredding service with certificate
- Burning (in extreme cases only)

Electronic Documents:
- Secure deletion with overwrite
- Deletion from all backup systems
- Deletion from cloud storage
- Professional data destruction service

Media:
- Physical destruction of CDs/DVDs
- Hard drive wiping or physical destruction
- USB drive wiping or physical destruction

IMPORTANT - Litigation Hold:
----------------------------
If litigation is anticipated or pending, ALL document disposal must STOP for
documents related to the matter. Consult legal counsel before resuming disposal.
