CONVERSION OPTIMIZATION
================================================================================

Service Request Flow:
  1. Entry points: Hero CTA, crisis banner, floating buttons
  2. Urgency triage: Emergency → Call | Today → Form | Week → Schedule
  3. Minimal form: 5-7 fields, optional name, visual checkboxes
  4. Confirmation: Clear next steps, privacy assurance

Donation Flow:
  1. Context-aware: After reading about services
  2. Impact framing: $25 = 10 naloxone kits
  3. Simple form: Preset amounts, custom option
  4. Legal disclosure: 501(c)(3), EIN, tax-deductible language
  5. Thank you: Social share options

Crisis Intervention:
  1. Phone: tel: link → Native dialer → One tap
  2. Text: sms: link → Messaging app → Pre-filled message
  3. Web: Form → Urgency triage → Emergency redirects to phone
