Our Services

All services are free, confidential, and provided without judgment. We meet you where you are.

Overdose Prevention

Free naloxone (Narcan) kits, fentanyl test strips, and overdose response training. Life-saving supplies available anytime.

  • Naloxone distribution
  • Fentanyl test strips
  • Overdose response education
  • Safer use supplies

Syringe Exchange

Sterile syringes, safer injection supplies, sharps disposal, and harm reduction education to prevent disease transmission.

  • Sterile syringe access
  • Safe disposal containers
  • Injection safety education
  • Safer sex supplies

Wound Care

Basic wound assessment, first aid supplies, wound care kits, and referrals to medical care when needed.

  • Wound assessment
  • First aid supplies
  • Wound care kits
  • Medical referrals

Health Screening

Blood pressure checks, glucose testing, HIV/HCV rapid testing coordination, and linkage to healthcare services.

  • Blood pressure screening
  • Glucose testing
  • HIV/HCV testing referrals
  • Primary care connection

Peer Support

One-on-one peer support from people with lived experience. Crisis de-escalation, emotional support, and connection to resources.

  • Peer-based support
  • Crisis navigation
  • Treatment referrals
  • Mental health linkage

Housing & ID Support

Housing navigation, shelter referrals, ID replacement assistance, and connection to community resources.

  • Housing navigation
  • Shelter referrals
  • ID replacement help
  • Resource connection

Cultural Healing

Indigenous-informed healing circles, culturally safe engagement, and traditional wellness practices integrated into care.

  • Healing circles
  • Cultural safety
  • Indigenous practices
  • Community connection

Education & Training

Community overdose prevention training, harm reduction workshops, and education on safer substance use practices.

  • Naloxone training
  • Harm reduction education
  • Community workshops
  • Peer educator support

Resource Navigation

Connection to food banks, legal aid, employment services, and community resources to support overall well-being.

  • Food & clothing access
  • Legal aid referrals
  • Employment support
  • Benefits navigation

Community in Action

Serving communities with dignity, compassion, and culturally-informed care

At Healing Roots, we believe that genuine connection is the foundation of healing. Our peer-led outreach creates safe spaces where community members feel seen, heard, and valued‚Äîregardless of where they are in their journey.

Through consistent presence and authentic relationship-building, we're breaking down barriers to care and fostering trust within communities that have historically been underserved and marginalized.

Our peer navigators bring lived experience to every interaction. They understand the challenges firsthand and offer judgment-free support that meets people exactly where they are‚Äîwhether that's crisis intervention, treatment navigation, or simply a listening ear.

This peer-to-peer approach creates authentic connections that formal services often can't replicate, building bridges to resources and hope when they're needed most.

Healing happens across generations. Our Indigenous-informed approach honors the wisdom of elders while empowering youth to become leaders in their own communities. This intergenerational exchange strengthens cultural identity and builds resilience.

By weaving together traditional knowledge and modern harm reduction practices, we create holistic pathways to wellness that respect the whole person‚Äîmind, body, spirit, and community.
