Rooted in Community & Culture

Lilly's journey reflects deep commitment to Indigenous sovereignty, cultural preservation, and community-centered healing. With extensive experience in cultural organizing, peer support, and wellness advocacy, Lilly brings holistic perspectives to HROC's work.

Lilly understands that healing from trauma - including the collective trauma of colonization, systemic racism, and marginalization - requires cultural wisdom, community connection, and respect for Indigenous knowledge systems.
