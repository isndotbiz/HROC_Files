## ğŸ‘ï¸ Public Disclosure Requirements {#public-disclosure}

### Documents You MUST Make Available

You are **legally required** to provide these documents to anyone who requests them:

1. **Form 1023** (Application for Tax-Exempt Status)
2. **IRS Determination Letter**
3. **Last 3 Years of Form 990** Returns

### How to Comply

**Best Practice:** Post on your website! This satisfies most disclosure requirements.

**In-Person Requests:**
- Provide immediately or by next business day
- Can charge reasonable copying fees
- Must allow inspection at your office

**Written Requests:**
- Must provide within 30 days
- Can charge copying and postage

**Penalties for Non-Compliance:**
- $20/day for each day late
- Up to $10,000 per return
- No maximum for exemption application

**âœ… Recommended:** Add a "Transparency" page to your website with all required documents.
