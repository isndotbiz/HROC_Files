## ğŸ¯ What is the SPV? {#what-is-spv}

**Healing Roots Outreach SPV LLC** is a single-member limited liability company wholly owned by HROC.

**Purpose:**
- Manage RV/vehicle leasing for mobile harm reduction services
- Protect HROC's 501(c)(3) status from vehicle-related liabilities
- Provide clear separation for grant compliance

**Legal Structure:**
- **Type:** Single-Member LLC (SMLLC)
- **Owner:** Healing Roots Outreach Collective (100%)
- **Manager:** Jonathan Mallinger
- **Tax Status:** Disregarded entity (flows through to HROC's Form 990)

## ğŸš— Vehicle Operations {#vehicle-ops}

### Daily Requirements

**Mileage Logs:**
- Program staff must complete daily mileage logs
- SPV Manager verifies monthly
- Include: Date, driver, starting/ending odometer, purpose, miles

**Pre-Trip Inspection:**
- Visual inspection before each use
- Check: tires, lights, fluids, safety equipment
- Document any issues immediately

### Maintenance Schedule

**Routine Maintenance:**
- Oil changes: Every 3,000-5,000 miles or as recommended
- Tire rotation: Every 5,000-7,500 miles
- Inspections: Follow manufacturer schedule
- **Document all maintenance** in vehicle file

**Repairs:**
- Get approval for repairs >$500 (dual signature requirement)
- Keep all receipts and invoices
- Update maintenance log immediately
- Store records for 7 years

### Authorized Drivers

**Requirements:**
- Valid driver's license
- Clean driving record check
- Completed driver training
- Added to insurance policy
- Signed vehicle use agreement

**Update List Quarterly:**

- Verify licenses still valid
- Update insurance
