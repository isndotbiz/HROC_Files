## Appendix: Detailed Requirements {#appendix}

### A. Form 990 Filing Details

**Form Type Selection:**

| Gross Receipts | Form Required | Complexity |
|----------------|---------------|------------|
| ≤ $50,000 | 990-N (e-Postcard) | Simplest - online only, ~10 minutes |
| $50,001 - $200,000 | 990-EZ | Moderate - 4 pages + schedules |
| > $200,000 | 990 (Full) | Complex - 12+ pages + schedules |

**First Year Filing (2025 tax year):**
- HROC incorporated July 17, 2025
- Fiscal year 2025 = July 17 - Dec 31, 2025 (short year)
- File Form 990 by **May 15, 2026**
- Likely Form 990-EZ or 990-N (depending on receipts in 5.5 months)

**SPV Reporting:**
- SPV LLC is disregarded entity (flows through to HROC)
- Report ALL SPV income/expenses on HROC Form 990
- Complete **Schedule R** (Related Organizations)
- Identify SPV as "disregarded entity"
- Consolidate financials (HROC + SPV = one Form 990)

**Where to File:**
- **990-N:** Online only at https://www.irs.gov/charities-non-profits/annual-electronic-filing-requirement-for-small-exempt-organizations-form-990-n-e-postcard
- **990-EZ/990:** E-file through IRS-approved vendor OR mail to IRS (Ogden, UT)

**Extensions:**
- File Form 8868 by May 15 to get automatic 6-month extension
- Extended deadline: November 16, 2026
- NO SECOND EXTENSION AVAILABLE
- Extension is to file, not to pay (if any tax due)

**Penalties:**
- $20/day for late filing (small orgs)
- Up to $10,000 per return
- **CRITICAL:** Missing 3 consecutive years = automatic loss of 501(c)(3) status

**Post-Filing Requirements:**
- Post on website within 30 days of filing
- Make available for public inspection upon request
- Keep copy permanently

**IRS Form 990 Resources:**
- [Return Due Dates for Exempt Organizations](https://www.irs.gov/charities-non-profits/return-due-dates-for-exempt-organizations-annual-return)
- [E-file for Charities and Non-Profits](https://www.irs.gov/e-file-providers/e-file-for-charities-and-non-profits)
- [Form 990 Due Date Calculator](https://www.tax990.com/form-990-filing-due-date/)

### J. Public Disclosure Compliance

**Documents You MUST Provide Upon Request:**

1. **Form 1023** (Application for Exemption)
2. **IRS Determination Letter**
3. **Form 990s** (last 3 years)

**How to Provide:**

**Option 1 - Website Posting (BEST PRACTICE):**
- Post all required documents on HROC website
- Create "Transparency" or "Public Documents" page
- Satisfies most disclosure requirements automatically
- Update annually with new Form 990

**Option 2 - In-Person Inspection:**
- Must allow inspection at principal office during business hours
- Must provide immediately or by next business day
- Can charge reasonable copying fees

**Option 3 - Written Request:**
- Must provide within 30 days of written request
- Can charge:
  - Actual copying costs (per page)
  - Actual postage
  - No labor charges allowed

**Redactions Allowed:**
- Donor names/addresses (Schedule B) - if <2% of support
- Trade secrets (rare for nonprofits)

**Penalties for Non-Compliance:**
- $20/day for each day request not fulfilled
- Up to $10,000 per Form 990
- No maximum for Form 1023

**Best Practice:**
- Post on website to avoid requests altogether
- Keep log of any requests received
- Respond within 5 business days
