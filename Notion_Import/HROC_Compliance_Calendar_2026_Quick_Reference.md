## FISCAL YEAR INFORMATION

**HROC Fiscal Year:** Calendar Year (January 1 - December 31)

**Why Calendar Year?**
- Standard for nonprofits (easier IRS alignment)
- Simplifies donor tax receipts
- Easier grant reporting
- Form 990 due May 15 following year-end

## IMPORTANT NOTES

**Form 990 Requirements:**
- Receipts ≤ $50,000: File 990-N (e-Postcard)
- Receipts $50,001 - $200,000: File 990-EZ
- Receipts > $200,000: File 990 (Full)

**SPV Reporting:**
- SPV is disregarded entity (single-member LLC)
- ALL SPV income/expenses reported on HROC Form 990
- Complete Schedule R (Related Organizations)
- Consolidate financials (HROC + SPV = one Form 990)

**Public Disclosure:**
- Must post on website: Form 1023, Determination Letter, last 3 Form 990s
- Or provide within 30 days of written request
- Penalty: $20/day for late response

**BOI Report (FinCEN):**
- As of March 2025, U.S.-formed LLCs are EXEMPT
- No BOI report required for HROC or SPV
- Only foreign entities must file
